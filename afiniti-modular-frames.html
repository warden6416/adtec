AFN-1000 Frame

The space-saving & power-efficient AFN-1000 frame was designed with industry evolution in mind. Each frame includes a system host carrier with on-board IP multiplexing capabilities, a web-based user interface and front panel controls for network configuration. This flexible platform can accommodate seven additional function-based carriers enabling customized workflows.

AFN-250 Frame

The AFN-250 frame provides a compact and flexible design for in-house or on-the-go applications. Designed for professional video streaming, multiplexing and turn-around applications, this frame allows users to meet current needs and budgets. When needed, users can expand to the AFN-1000 frame as all carriers are interchangeable within the afiniti family.
