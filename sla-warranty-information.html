SLA & Warranty Information

Service Level Agreements

Adtec is committed to service after the sale. From on-site installation and integration services to after-hour and special event support, we offer standard and optional Service Level Agreements to fit your needs. These plans can be purchased at time of original sale or after.

Standard SLA

Included with all current products during active product life.

Enhanced SLA

Extended coverage with priority response times. Contact slaquestions@adtecinc.com for quote.

Legacy SLA

Available for most End of Life Products. Contact slaquestions@adtecinc.com for quote.

All SLAs and warranties exclude equipment that has been subject to misuse, negligence, or accident and are subject to terms and conditions of Sale and Support.

30-Day Replacement Policy

In the event that a new Adtec manufactured product malfunctions within 30 days of original purchase, contact Adtec for failure verification and return authorization. If you purchased your new Adtec product through an authorized reseller, proof of purchase is required.
