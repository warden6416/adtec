End of Life Products

Products are discontinued for a variety of reasons including market demands, technology innovation, or natural product maturation.

Adtec will provide notice as to the serviceability of our products via the support section of our website. Access to technical support will be available through the purchase of a Legacy SLA for a period of 2 years from the end of life date for hardware and OS software issues, and 1 year for application software issues and firmware upgrades.

Documentation and downloads for end of life products will remain available on our website during that time.
